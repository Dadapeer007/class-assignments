Rajinikanth - The Superstar

About Rajinikanth

Rajinikanth, also known as Thalaivar, is one of the most iconic and beloved actors in Indian cinema. He was born on December 12, 1950, in Bangalore, India.

Rajinikanth has appeared in over 150 films, predominantly in Tamil cinema, and has a massive fan following not only in India but also worldwide.
